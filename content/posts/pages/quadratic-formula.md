+ It gives solution of a quadratic equation
$$
x_{1,2} = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}.
$$
+ Values of $a$, $b$, $c$ are from quadratic equation.
+ There might be no real-number solutions.
